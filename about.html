Blue Moon Finance Pvt. Ltd. is a student-developed banking management system designed to demonstrate modern banking operations through a secure and user-friendly digital platform. Our project aims to provide an efficient way to manage essential banking activities such as account management, deposits, withdrawals, fund transfers, and transaction tracking.

This project was proudly developed by Roshan Thapa Magar and Jaya Chaudhari as part of an academic initiative to apply software development, database management, and web technologies in a real-world banking scenario.

Our Vision

To understand real world Banking System's and implementing simple core functionalies of the Banking System's with Aesthetically pleasing UI.

Our Mission :

  • Provide a simple and effective banking platform
  • Understanding transaction processing.
  • Understanding and implementing 2FA.
  • Deliver a professional and user-friendly experience
